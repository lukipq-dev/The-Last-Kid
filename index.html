The Last Kid

After a sudden abduction, you wake up inside a mysterious house. Every door is locked. Every room hides a clue. Explore the house, search for keys, and slowly uncover the truth behind the person who brought you here.

About The Game

The Last Kid is a story-driven Roblox horror experience focused on mystery, exploration, and atmosphere. You play as a teenager who wakes up inside a strange house after being taken. Each room hides clues, puzzles, and fragments of a story that slowly reveal why you were brought here. As you explore deeper, the house begins to tell its story.

Game Features

Exploration

Search rooms, hidden spaces, and locked areas while slowly uncovering the truth.

Puzzles

Find keys, unlock doors, and solve environmental puzzles to progress.

Story

Piece together the mystery through notes, clues, and discoveries hidden throughout the house.

Atmosphere

Dark rooms, quiet halls, and an unsettling feeling that you're not alone.
